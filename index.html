Common Questions

Is this a custodial service? +
No. KaspaWoo is 100% non-custodial. Your private keys never leave your wallet. Payments go directly from the customer's wallet to your own Kaspa address.
What are the fees? +
There are zero fees from KaspaWoo. You only pay the standard Kaspa network transaction fees, which are typically less than a fraction of a cent.
Which wallets are supported? +
Any Kaspa wallet that allows you to export an Extended Public Key (KPUB/XPUB) is supported. This includes popular wallets like Kaspium and the Kaspa Web Wallet.
How long does it take to set up? +
Setup takes less than 5 minutes. Simply install the plugin, import your KPUB, and you're ready to start accepting KAS on your WooCommerce store.
Is it secure? +
Yes. By using a "watch-only" wallet (KPUB), your website can generate new receiving addresses but cannot spend your funds. Even if your store is compromised, your funds remain safe in your cold wallet.
