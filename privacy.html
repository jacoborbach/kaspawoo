Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 30, 2026

1. Overview

KaspaWoo is a non-custodial software solution for WordPress/WooCommerce. We believe in privacy and security by design.

2. Data Collection

Private Keys: The KaspaWoo plugin does NOT collect, store, or transmit your private keys. Your keys stay in your wallet application.

Transaction Data: All transaction monitoring happens on the public Kaspa blockchain. KaspaWoo does not store your customers' personal information on any external servers; all data remains within your own WordPress database.

Website Analytics: This landing page may use minimal analytics to improve the user experience, but we do not track or sell individual user data.

3. Non-Custodial Nature

Because KaspaWoo uses watch-only (KPUB) technology, it is physically impossible for the software to spend your funds. You are the sole custodian of your cryptocurrency.

4. Third-Party Services

The plugin may interact with public Kaspa nodes or the CoinGecko API to fetch exchange rates. These services have their own privacy policies.
